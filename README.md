# Music Player
KS4x Music Player made by KS4xZYX.
Wait music player to download built-in songs, after that the buttons and stuff will work.
The music player does not play roblox sounds from the creator store, it is executor workspace based.

loadstring:
```lua
loadstring(game:HttpGet("https://github.com/ks4xzyx-spec/KS4x/raw/refs/heads/Music-Player/MPSource.lua"))()
```
